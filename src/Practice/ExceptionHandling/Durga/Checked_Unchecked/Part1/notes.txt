Checked Exceptions vs Unchecked Exceptions :
---------------------------------------------------
Checked-Exception:
----------------------
-> The Exceptions which are checked by the compiler whether programmer is handling it or not, then these Exceptions
are known as Checked-Exceptions.
Examples : FileNotFound-Exception, InterruptedExceptions........

UnChecked-Exception:
-------------------------
-> Exception which are not checked or alerted by compiler and occur at Runtime, those are Unchecked-Exceptions.
Examples : ArithmeticException


***) Note : "Error" and its Child classes are Unchecked.
            "Runtime-Exception" and its child classes are Unchecked-Exceptions, remaining all other Exceptions are CheckedExceptions.
