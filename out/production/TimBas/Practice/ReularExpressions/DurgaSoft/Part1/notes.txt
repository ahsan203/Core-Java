If we want to represent a group of String-Objects according to some patter, then we use Regular Expression.

In Regular Expression we have 2 important classes from java.util.regex package:
                                                                                (1) Pattern class
                                                                                (2) Matcher class
Important methods :
-------------------------------------
1)Pattern p = Pattern.compile("specify-pattern");
2)Matcher m = p.matcher("Target-pattern");
3)m.find()
4)m.stat()
5)m.end()
6)m.group()
