throw keyword:
---------------

1) Sometimes we can create Exception-Object explicitly and handover to JVM manually, for this we ue "throw" keyword.
                throw new ArithmeticException("/ by zero")

2) Hence the main objective of throw keyword is, to hand over our created Exception-Object to JVM manually.

3) To create Customized Exception Object we use throw-keyword.
